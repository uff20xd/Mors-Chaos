A technique to create a reality with your soul as the base. Inside this new reality called an Oktae and its effects are dependant on your innate abilities and personality. Each Oktae is personal and unique.
Because the Oktae is based on your soul you can use 100% of your potential and cits the only way to use the unlimited gamma function.

The full incantation is "Soul Inversion, {Sign}, Velto Oktae Dimitra".

To use a Soul inversion Velto Oktae dimitra the user needs to assign a "Sign" to their soul which can not be changed. This sign decides about 50% of the behavior but its dependant on the soul itself.

Signs:
Sign of the Pheonix - Mostly effects the users body changing it in some way also grants a stat boost of 20% to all stats beyond maximum potential.

Sign of the World - Overwrites reality instead of creating an isolated one to manipulate it in some kind also can not choose its targets but still can decide range an if it wants a barrirr or not.

Sign of the Basilisk - Directly has an effect on other people inside the Oktae




Some signs have alternate Versions like "Sign of the Blue Pheonix" used by Endima and combines the effects of "Sign of the Pheonix" and "Sign of the World".

Special Signs:

Sign of the Fool - This sign is one of a kind and used only by zeero. Its effect is to perfect the techiques of the fool to create a perfect Oktae. ---> True Neutral

Sign of the Cosmos - Creates an exact copy of the universe the user originaly resided in to apply its effects. Similar to the sign of the World but at 50% efficiency. A cheap copy.
